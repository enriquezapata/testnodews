7.1.5.5. Private Sewers and Private water Supply

(1) Private water supply pipes shall be designed and installed according to MOE PIBS 6881e, "design Guidelines for Drinking-water Systems".

(2) Private sewersshall be designed and installed according to MOE PIBS 6879, "design Guidelines for Sewage Works".
