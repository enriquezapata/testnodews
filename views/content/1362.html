The Ontario Building Code | Application

9.9.3.1. application

(1) This Subsection applies to every means of egress except exits that serve not more than one dwelling unit and access to exits within dwelling units.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Sentence 9.9.3.1.(1) of Division B of the Regulation is revoked and the following substituted: (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 90)

(1) This Subsection applies to every means of egress except,

(a) exitsthat serve a house or an individual dwelling unit, and

(b) access to exits within a house or an individual dwelling unit.
