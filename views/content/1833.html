9.23.6.3. Anchorage of Smaller buildings

(1) Buildings not more than 4.3 m wide and not more than 1 storey in building height are permitted to be anchored in conformance with the requirements of CSA Z240.10.1, "Site Preparation, Foundation and Anchorage of Manufactured Homes".
