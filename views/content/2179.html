The Ontario Building Code | Application

9.35.1.1. application

(1) This Section applies to garages and carports serving a single dwelling unit.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Sentence 9.35.1.1.(1) of Division B of the Regulation is amended by striking out "single dwelling unit" at the end and substituting "house or an individual dwelling unit". (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 132)
