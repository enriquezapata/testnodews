The Ontario Building Code | Application

9.9.11.1. application

(1) This Subsection applies to all exits except those serving not more than one dwelling unit.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Sentence 9.9.11.1.(1) of Division B of the Regulation is amended by striking out "not more than one dwelling unit" at the end and substituting "a house or an individual dwelling unit". (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 97)
