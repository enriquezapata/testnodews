3.7.1.4. Sleeping Areas in Camps

(1) Recreational camps shall have an area in the sleeping quarters of not less than 3.72 m2per camper or, if double or triple tier bunk units are used, 2.79 m2per camper.

(2) A camp for housing of workers shall have an area of not less than 3.72 m2 per employee in every room used for sleeping purposes.
