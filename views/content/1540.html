9.10.20.2. Access to basements

(1) Except in basements serving not more than one dwelling unit, each unsprinklered basementexceeding 25 m in length or width shall be provided with direct access to the outdoorref ="http://thehandyforce.com/doors/" title ="Toronto Door installer">doors to at least one street.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Sentence 9.10.20.2.(1) of Division B of the Regulation is amended by striking out "Except in basements serving not more than one dwelling unit" at the beginning and substituting "Except in basements of houses". (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 119)

(2) Access required in Sentence (1) is permitted to be provided by a door, window or other means that provides an opening not less than 1 100 mm high and 550 mm wide, the sill height of which shall not be more than 900 mm above the floor.

(3) Access required in Sentence (1) is also permitted to be provided by an interior stair accessible from the outdoorref ="http://thehandyforce.com/doors/" title ="Toronto Door installer">doors.
