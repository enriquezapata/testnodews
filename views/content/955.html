The Ontario Building Code | Copper Tube

7.2.7.4. copper Tube

(1) copper tube in a plumbing system shall,

(a) be certified to ASTM B88, "Seamless copper Water tube", or

(b) comply with ASTM B306, "copper Drainage tube (DWV)".

(2) The use of copper tube shall conform to Table 7.2.7.4.

(3) copper tube used in a plumbing applianceshall conform to,

(a) ASTM B88, "Seamless copper Water tube", or

(b) ASTM B68 / B68M, "Seamless copper Tube, Bright Annealed".

(4) Type K or L copper tube shall be used for the potablewater side of a heat exchanger in a pre-engineered wastewater heat recovery system.
