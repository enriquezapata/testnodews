The Ontario Building Code | Height over Ramps

9.8.5.3. Height over Ramps

(1) The clear height over ramps shall be not less than,

(a) 1 950 mm for ramps serving a single dwelling unit, and

Note: On July 1, 2017, Clause 9.8.5.3.(1)(a) of Division B of the Regulation is revoked and the following substituted: (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 79)

(a) 1 950 mm for ramps serving a house or an individual dwelling unit, and

(b) 2 050 mm for ramps not serving a single dwelling unit.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Clause 9.8.5.3.(1)(b) of Division B of the Regulation is revoked and the following substituted: (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 79)

(b) 2 050 mm for ramps not serving a house or an individual dwelling unit.
