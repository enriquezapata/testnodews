7.3.2.7. Cold-Caulked Joints

(1) Cold-caulked joints shall not be used except for bell and spigot pipe in a water system, a drainage system or a venting system.

(2) The caulking compound used in cold-caulked joints shall be applied according to the manufacturerâ€™s directions.

(3) Every cold-caulked joint in a drainage systemshall be firmly packed with oakum and tightly caulked with cold caulking compound to a depth of at least 25 mm.
