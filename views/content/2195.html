9.36.3.1. buildings for Seasonal Tourist Accommodation or for Rent

(1) Wherebuildings are used or intended to be used for seasonal tourist accommodation or for rent, they shall comply with Sections 9.5. to 9.8. in addition to the requirements of this Section.
