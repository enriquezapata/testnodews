3.1.16.1. Fabric Awnings, Canopies and Marquees

(1) Fabrics used as part of an awning, canopyor marquee that is located within or attached to a building of any type of construction shall conform to CAN/ulc-S109, "Flame Tests of Flame-Resistant Fabrics and Films".
