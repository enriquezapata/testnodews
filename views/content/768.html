4.3.5.1. design Basis for Aluminium

(1) buildings and their structural members made of aluminum shall conform to CAN/CSA-S157 / S157.1, "Strength design in Aluminum/Commentary on CSA S157-05, Strength design in Aluminum", using the loads stipulated in Section 4.1., in accordance with limit states design in Subsection 4.1.3.
