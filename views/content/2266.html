The Ontario Building Code | Residential Furnaces After December 31, 2014

12.3.1.5. residential Furnaces After December 31, 2014

(1) Sentence (2) applies to construction for which a permit has been applied for after December 31, 2014.

(2) A furnace serving a dwelling unit shall be equipped with a brushless direct current motor.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Article 12.3.1.5. of Division B of the Regulation is revoked and the following substituted: (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 146)

12.3.1.5. residential Furnaces

(1) A furnace serving a house or an individual dwelling unit shall be equipped with a brushless direct current motor.
