9.32.2.1. Natural ventilation Area

(1) The unobstructed openable ventilation area to the doors for rooms and spaces in residential buildings ventilated by natural means shall conform to Table 9.32.2.1.

Note: On July 1, 2017, Sentence 9.32.2.1.(1) of Division B of the Regulation is amended by striking out "residential buildings" and substituting "a residential occupancy". (See: O. Reg. 139/17, s. 126)
