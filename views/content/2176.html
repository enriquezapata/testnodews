9.34.2.6. garages and Carports

(1) A lighting="http://thehandyforce.com/electrical/" title ="East York Electrician">lighting outlet with fixture shall be provided for an attached, built-in or detached garage or carport.

(2) Except as provided in Sentence (3), lighting="http://thehandyforce.com/electrical/" title ="East York Electrician">lighting outlets required in Sentence (1) shall be controlled by a wall switch near the doorway ="http://thehandyforce.com/doors/" title ="Toronto Door installer">doorway.

(3) Where the lighting="http://thehandyforce.com/electrical/" title ="East York Electrician">lighting outlet and fixture required in Sentence (1) are ceiling mounted above an area not normally occupied by a parked car, or are wall mounted, a fixture with a built-in switch is permitted to be used.

(4) Where a carport is lighted by a light at the entrance to a dwelling unit, additional carport lighting="http://thehandyforce.com/electrical/" title ="East York Electrician">lighting is not required.
